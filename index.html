Our story

Haukai Restaurant specialises in Māori kai and provides an all round Te Ao Māori experience.

The founders Wiremu (Ngā Puhi iwi) and Sonya (Te Rawara iwi) are Bay of Islands natives and are passionate about keeping the Maori culture alive through Kai.
